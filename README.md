# Retail Sales & Profitability Analysis (SQL)

## Objective
Analyze retail sales data to evaluate revenue performance, profitability, and regional trends.

## Tools Used
- SQL (MySQL)
- MS Excel

## Dataset
- Generated 10,000+ synthetic records using SQL
- Includes order details such as region, product, customer ID, quantity, price, and planned revenue

## Analysis Performed

### 1. Total Revenue
Calculated overall revenue using quantity and price

### 2. Monthly Revenue Trend
Analyzed month-wise revenue performance

### 3. Revenue Growth
Measured month-over-month revenue growth using window functions

### 4. Plan vs Actual Analysis
Compared planned revenue with actual revenue across regions

### 5. Regional Performance
Ranked regions based on revenue performance

### 6. Product Performance
Analyzed top-performing products by quantity and revenue

## Key Insights
- Revenue varies across regions with some regions outperforming others  
- Month-over-month growth highlights business performance trends  
- Variance between planned and actual revenue indicates performance gaps  
- Certain products contribute significantly to total revenue
